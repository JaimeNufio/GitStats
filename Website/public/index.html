Danceability:

How suitable a song is for dancing based on tempo, rhythm, beat, and how consistent those are throughout the track.

Energy:

Percieved intensity and activity. Energetic tracks are fast, loud, and noisey. Attributes that contribute include dynamic range, loudness, timbre, onset rate, and general entropy.

Valence:

Valence describes positivity in the track. A track with a high score is more associated with postive feelings, like happiness, while a track with a low score is more associated with negative feelings, like saddness.

Speechiness:

How much of the track is vocally focused, opposed to musically focused. This value is scaled here to see the values more closely. This value is actually much closer to a binary yes or no.

Loudness:

How loud the track is. This scale starts around -20dB and goes upto 0dB. Loudness is correlated to physical power of the song, as in amplitude.
