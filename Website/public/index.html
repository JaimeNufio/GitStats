How suitable a song is for dancing based on tempo, rhythm, beat, and how consistent those are throughout the track.

Percieved intensity and activity. Energetic tracks are fast, loud, and noisey. Attributes that contribute include dynamic range, loudness, timbre, onset rate, and general entropy.

Valence

Valence describes positivity in the track. A track with a high score is more associated with postivity, such as feelings of happiness, cheerfulness, and euphoria. A track with a low score is more associated with negative feelings, such as saddness, anger, and depression.
